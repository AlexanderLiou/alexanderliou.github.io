Stereolithography 3D Printer Small Batch Material Testing Apparatus

This apparatus interfaces with industrial SLA 3D printers to downsize their operations for small batch material testing. It taps into existing printer feedback loops to achieve high print resolution via custom resin-handling mechanisms. The device saves 98% of testing material and a predicted 90% of production material cost.
