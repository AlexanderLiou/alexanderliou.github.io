Stereolithography 3D Printer Small Batch Material Testing Apparatus

This apparatus interfaces with industrial SLA 3D printers to downsize their operations for small batch material testing. Princeton Racing Electric FSAE Car Side Pods

I designed these sidepods for Princeton Racing Electric's FSAE car. They are mounted to the sides of the car in order to manipulate airflow to cool motor radiators and produce downforce that increases traction and cornering performance. Inverted fiberglass layup airfoils mounted between vertical plates produce downward aerodynamic force, and radiators behind the foils cool liquid dissipating heat from electric drive motors.

Swerve Drive Robot

This small scale swerve drive robot was built as a circuit analysis class final project with a partner (Jackie Liu). Controlled by an Arduino, the robot uses positional rotation servo motors to direct the wheels in the correct direction and continuous rotation servos to drive the wheels in their respective headings. The controller uses an Arduino with a bluetooth transmitter, sending the gyroscope angles of the controller to the robot.

F4 CADathon Fall 2021 Robot Design, solo

I designed this FRC-style robot as my submission to a CADathon event and won 6th place overall. A CADathon is a short CAD competition where competitors design FRC robots to compete in hypothetical games. This robot features a 4-stage telescoping elevator that lifts tetrahedral game pieces onto goals to score points. It utilizes a vacuum pad mounted on an over-centering linkage to theoretically lift itself onto a platform.

F4 CADathon Summer 2021 Robot Design, solo

I designed this FRC-style robot solo, which scored 5th place in a CADathon. The CADathon required competitors to design a robot to play a hypothetical FRC game. It is designed to pick up and kick soccer balls into goals with a rolling intake. It has an elevator with an actuating arm for lifting steel bars with electromagnets, and the arm doubles to lift the robot to climb a structure at the end of a match. Statics calculations were done to ensure sufficient torque on mechanism motors.

F4 CADathon Summer 2020 Robot Design, team of 3

I designed this robot in a team of 3 for a CADathon. It scored 8th place overall and was designed to manipulate yoga balls and shoot them into goals. I CADed a large three stage telescoping elevator on the rear of the robot to manipulate balls on a high truss.

F4 CADathon Spring 2020 Robot Design, team of 3
