VTOL Mk 2 Tricopter Aircraft

Summary

This aircraft is a new design that improves upon the VTOL Mk1 aircraft. I wrote sizing scripts and used vortex lattice simulation methods to design the planform, while accommodating design considerations for hover mode and sizing propulsion components for maximum efficiency. The front two rotors tilt to transition to forward flight. The design justifications for this aircraft are grounded in aerodynamic fundamentals, stability considerations, and a goal of high efficiency, which is often a challenge for lift-cruise configurations. The report below lays out the analysis, simulation, and design completed during the semester.
